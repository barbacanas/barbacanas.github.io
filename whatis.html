What's Barbacanas Treausere

Barbicans is a kickstarter of a real adventure through different routes throughout Europe, where you can get prizes. These prizes are the treasures, and 2 million of the euros raised in the kickstarter will be for the adventure as treasures. There will be many prizes, some worth the cost of the adventure and others larger amount, for example the big prize will be worth half a million euros in gold, silver and precious stones.

Where? & when?

Where? will be throughout Europe, several different routes with different prizes for the routes. When? in June 2023

How to register?

On the main page there is a shortcut to the kickstarter page. For € 1 you can register and we give you the ebook for the tests and routes, there will also be websites with problems and monitoring (if they want to appear on the web).
